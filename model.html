How OceanFlow works

What the problem is, what our solution is, and how the open loop works in practice.

1. THE PROBLEM

Data centers need stronger cooling without leaning so hard on freshwater.

Traditional pressure points

  • AI hardware generates intense heat loads
  • Air-heavy cooling becomes less effective at high density
  • Freshwater use is becoming costly and controversial

What operators need

A system that is scalable, easier to defend environmentally, and more aligned with long-term AI infrastructure demand.

2. THE SOLUTION

OceanFlow turns seawater into a cooling advantage.

3. THE FLOW

Step-by-step summary of our solution

1

Water enters

Seawater is drawn in from a coastal location.

2

Water is cleaned

Filtration helps manage sand, sediment, and particulates.

3

Heat is transferred

A corrosion-resistant exchanger moves heat away without exposing servers directly to seawater.

4

Servers stay cooler

The internal loop supports more stable operation in high-density computing environments.

5

Water is managed responsibly

Return or discharge is controlled to fit thermal and environmental safeguards.
